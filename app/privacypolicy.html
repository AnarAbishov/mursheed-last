PRIVACY POLICY FOR CLIENTS

Thank you for using Mursheed! Your trust is important to us and we’re committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. The information that’s shared with us helps us to provide a great experience with Mursheed.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and disclose your personal information, in conjunction with your access to and use of the Mursheed System. This privacy policy describes our privacy practices for all websites, platforms and services that link to it. Please read the privacy policy below.

1. INTRODUCTION

“Ameer Group” LLC (hereinafter referred as the “Company”) is a limited liability company duly incorporated under the laws of the Republic of Azerbaijan with its principal place of business situated in Azerbaijan, is the controller of personal data of clients.

Mursheed’s Applications and websites (hereinafter referred as “Mursheed”) are the sole property of the Company.

2. DATA COLLECTION

2.1. Mursheed collects the following data in order to process your registration:

  • Client’s ID and contact details (such as phone numbers, emails and etc.);
  • Client’s geo-location and driving routes while using the application;
  • Information about the payment;
  • Client's ratings;
  • Information about disputes.
  • Identification data of the device on which the Bolt app has been installed.

2.2. The personal data is collected for the purpose of connecting clients with drivers to help them move around the places more efficiently.

2.3. The clients may disclose their geo-location to drivers while using the Mursheed application. The Mursheed application will not use clients’ geo-location unless the Mursheed application is used by the clients.

2.4. Mursheed displays client's photo, name, number and details of the voyage for the drivers to identify the clients.

2.5. Mursheed uses the information, which may include personal data, in order to prevent fraud and other illegal or infringing activities while using Mursheed. Mursheed can use personal data for risk assessment and security purposes, including the authentication of users. For these purposes, personal data may be shared with third parties, such as law enforcement authorities as permitted by applicable law.

3. LEGAL BASIS

3.1. Mursheed relies on the legal basis that the processing of client’s personal data is necessary for the performance of client’s agreement with Mursheed. If the client does not provide the requested information, Mursheed cannot or allow the respective client to administer and manage the services, nor can Mursheed provide customer service to that respective client.

3.2. When using personal data to serve commercial business interest of the Company, Mursheed will always balance clients’ rights and interests in the protection of their information against Mursheed’s rights and interests.

3.3. Personal data may be processed on the ground of legitimate interest in investigating and detecting fraudulent payments.

4. DATA SHARING

4.1. Mursheed will only disclose client’s personal data to drivers, whose order has been accepted by the driver. Drivers will see client's name, ratings, phone number, photo and current location data.

4.2. Depending on the location of the client, the personal data may be disclosed to the Mursheed’s representatives, affiliates, agents etc​. Processing of personal data by will occur under the same conditions as established in this privacy policy.

4.3. Mursheed does not sell or rent obtained personal data to any third parties.

5. SECURITY AND ACCESS

5.1. Any personal data collected in the course of providing services is transferred to and stored in the data centers of the Company.

5.2. The Company observes reasonable procedures to prevent unauthorised access to, and the misuse of, information including personal data. The Company uses appropriate business systems and procedures to protect and safeguard information including the obtained personal data. The Company further uses security procedures and technical and physical restrictions for accessing and using the personal data on date centers of the Company. Only authorised personnel of the Company has access to the personal data and they may access the data only for the purpose of resolving issues associated with the use of the services (including disputes regarding all the Mursheed services).

6. DRIVERS PERSONAL DATA

6.1. Clients may not process the personal data of drivers without the permission of the Company.

6.2. Clients may not contact any drivers or collect, record, store, grant access, use or cross-use the personal data provided by the drivers or accessible to a the client via the Mursheed for any reason other than for the purposes of fulfilling all the Mursheed services.

7. DATA RETENTION

7.1. The personal data of the clients will be stored as long as the respective client has an active account in Mursheed applications.

7.2. The Company will retain your information, which may include personal data, for as long as the Company deem it necessary to enable you to use any Mursheed Services, to provide these Services to you, to comply with applicable laws, resolve disputes with any parties and otherwise as necessary to allow the Company to conduct its business, including to detect and prevent fraud or other illegal activities.

7.3. All personal data the Company retains will be subject to this Privacy Policy. If you have a question about a specific retention period for certain types of personal data we process about you, please contact us via the contact details included below.

7.4. If client’s account will be closed, the personal data will be stored for additional 1 (one) year period.

7.5. The Company may store the data necessary for accounting purposes for 8 (eight) years.

7.6. In the event of suspicions of an administrational or criminal offence, fraud or false information, the data shall be stored for 10 (ten) years. In the event of disputes, the data shall be retained until the claim is satisfied or the expiry date of such claims.

7.7. The Company will respond to the request for deleting personal data submitted by an e-mail within 2 (two) months and specify the period of data deletion.

8. RIGHTS REGARDING PERSONAL DATA

8.1. The Company stores the registered personal data in its data centers. Any of the clients may view and amend his/her information through the Mursheed System.

8.2. The Company relies on the clients to ensure that his/her personal data is complete, accurate and current.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION AND GOVERNING LAW

9.1. Any disputes and disagreement arising from this Policy or dedicated to it, will be settled mainly by negotiations between the clients and the Company. Disputes relating to the processing of personal data are resolved through customer support of the Company.

9.2. Any disputes which cannot be settled by the way of negotiations regarding to this Policy shall be settled in the respective courts of the Republic of Azerbaijan within the scope of Azerbaijani law.
